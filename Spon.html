50 Odyssey of the Mind Spontaneous

  • You discover a door that leads to a world made entirely of candy. What do you do?
  • If animals could talk, which one would be the funniest, and what would it say?
  • You are stranded on a desert island and can only bring three items. What would you bring and why?
  • You invent a new holiday. What is it called, and how is it celebrated?
  • A tree in your yard can now talk. What does it say to you, and how do you respond?
  • If you could switch lives with any famous person for a day, who would it be and why?
  • You find a magic mirror that shows your future. What does it show, and how do you feel about it?
  • You get to design a new planet. What does it look like, and what kind of creatures live there?
  • If your shoes could talk, what would they say about the places they’ve been?
  • You are an inventor who can create any object. What is your invention, and how does it help people?
  • Your pet can now speak. What does it tell you about its life?
  • If you could talk to one kind of animal, which one would you choose, and what would you ask it?
  • Imagine there are no computers or phones in the world. How would people stay connected?
  • You find a book that can answer any question. What’s the first question you ask?
  • You are the leader of a kingdom that only has one rule: kindness. How do you ensure everyone follows it?
  • You discover a new color. What would you call it, and how would you describe it to others?
  • A robot is hired to work in your school. What job would you give it?
  • You could live in any time period. Where and when would you live, and what would you do there?
  • A spaceship lands in your backyard. Who gets out, and what happens next?
  • You can create a new food. What’s it made of, and why is it so special?
  • You suddenly grow wings. What do you do with your new ability?
  • A new animal is discovered. Describe it, and explain where it lives.
  • If the Earth suddenly stopped spinning, what would happen?
  • You find a suitcase full of treasure, but there's one problem: it’s locked. What do you do to open it?
  • You are given the ability to read minds for one day. How would you use this power?
  • You are tasked with designing a new school. What makes it different from the schools today?
  • A giant cloud is about to land on Earth. What should everyone do to prepare?
  • You can teleport anywhere. Where would you go first, and why?
  • You are given three wishes, but you cannot use them for yourself. What do you wish for?
  • A character in your favorite book is now real. How does it change your life?
  • You find a hidden cave, and inside it is an ancient treasure chest. What do you do next?
  • You can only use one word to describe yourself. What is it and why?
  • You discover a secret passage in your school. Where does it lead?
  • You are a superhero with the power to control weather. How do you use your power to help others?
  • You could only live in one season for the rest of your life. Which one would you pick and why?
  • You are on a quest to find the last piece of pizza in the world. What’s your plan?
  • A famous explorer wants to team up with you for an adventure. What do you explore together?
  • You can travel to any fictional world for one day. Where do you go, and what do you do?
  • You can only speak in rhymes for a day. How do you handle everyday situations?
  • You are a time traveler. What year would you visit, and what would you do there?
  • A mysterious box appears in front of you. What do you do with it, and what’s inside?
  • You are hired as the world's first "dream architect." What kind of dreams do you design?
  • You find a magical pen that makes anything you draw come to life. What do you draw first?
  • You discover a world where everything is upside down. How do you live in this new world?
  • You are given the power to create one new rule for the entire world. What is it?
  • You can speak to the stars. What do they say to you, and what do you ask them?
  • Your favorite cartoon character comes to life. What do you do together?
  • You’re given the power to shrink any object. What do you shrink first and why?
  • You’ve just won a contest to build the first treehouse in space. What does it look like?
  • You are the ruler of a world where everyone has the same dream. What’s the dream, and what happens when they wake up?
